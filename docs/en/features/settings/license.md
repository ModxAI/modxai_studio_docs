## Activation Process

### 1. Get a License Key
1. Visit the [ModxAI Website](https://www.modxai.net/pricing) or the in-app store.
2. Choose a plan that suits your needs.
3. After payment, a license key will be sent to your email.

### 2. Activate in App
1. Open ModxAI Studio.
2. Click the **Settings** icon in the sidebar.
3. Navigate to the **License** (or Authorization) section.
4. Click **Activate**.
5. Enter your license key.
6. Wait for validation to complete.

### 3. Activation Success
Once activated, you will see:
- **Status**: "Activated"
- **Plan**: Your plan type (e.g., Lifetime, Annual).
- **Expiration**: The date your license expires.

### Re-activation (Device Change)
If you reinstall the OS or make minor hardware changes (RAM/HDD):
- ‚úÖ The system automatically recognizes it as a recovery activation.
- ‚úÖ **Consumes an Activation Count**: Each key has a limit (e.g., max 10 re-activations).
- ‚úÖ Expiration date remains unchanged.

> üí° **Note**: Licenses are typically bound to a single device. Please keep your key secure.

## Free User Guidelines

### Quota System
Free users have monthly limits on:
- **Model Operations**: Number of loads/inferences.
- **Data Processing**: Number of dataset rows processed.
- **Training**: Number of checkpoints saved.

### Checking Quota
- Hover over the **Quota Indicator** in the top menu bar to see your remaining balance.

### Quota Exhaustion
When you run out of quota:
1. Features will show a "Quota Exceeded" warning.
2. You can wait for the next month's reset or upgrade to Pro.

### Network Requirement
> ‚ö†Ô∏è **Free users must remain online.**
- **Offline**: Features are disabled because quotas are managed server-side.
- **Unstable Network**: Operations may fail if the connection drops.

## Pro User Benefits

### Offline Usage
Pro users can work offline for extended periods:

| Plan Type | Max Offline Duration |
| :--- | :--- |
| Trial | 1 Day |
| Monthly | 10 Days |
| Quarterly | 30 Days |
| Yearly | 90 Days |
| Lifetime | 360 Days |

> üí° **How Offline Time Works**:
> *   Every time you connect to the internet, the offline timer **resets**.
> *   *Example*: A Lifetime user connects today; they can now stay offline for 360 days.
> *   We recommend connecting periodically to keep your license fresh.

### Expiration
If your subscription expires:
1. You will be notified to renew.
2. The account automatically reverts to **Free User** status.
3. You retain access to your data but are subject to quotas.

## Troubleshooting

### Activation Errors

| Error | Possible Cause | Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Invalid/Expired Key** | Typo or key already used. | Check input or contact support. |
| **Device Limit Reached** | Key used on too many devices. | Buy a new key or contact support. |
| **Network Error** | Cannot reach auth server. | Check internet connection. |

### Usage Issues

| Error | Possible Cause | Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Restricted Mode** | Local license needs validation. | Connect to internet to refresh. |
| **Quota Exceeded** | Free limit reached. | Wait for reset or upgrade. |
| **Offline Limit** | Exceeded max offline days. | Connect to internet. |
| **License Frozen** | Abnormal activity detected. | Contact support. |

### Quick Fixes
1. **Check Network**: Ensure you can access the web.
2. **Restart App**: Many temporary glitches resolve after a restart.
3. **Refresh License**: Go to Settings and click "Refresh" in the License section.
4. **Update App**: Ensure you are on the latest version.

## Security & Privacy

### Device Binding
- Licenses are bound to your hardware ID.
- Changing motherboards may require a new activation.

### VPN/Proxy Warning
> ‚ö†Ô∏è **Avoid using VPNs during activation.**

- If activation fails, try disabling your VPN/Proxy.

### Data Privacy
- ‚úÖ Communication is encrypted.
